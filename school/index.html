Design Overview

This is a school website designed to make academic processes easier, i looked back at most of the struggles i had in high school so i decided to tackel the issue with UX/UI processes from making personas to userflow, wireframing and prototype.

Pain Points & Solutions

  • Due to quick spread of rumours, students are mislead with wrong information.

    (Verified School news)

  • Most students either forget there was a homework or need valid details of the homework;

    (Homework reminder)

  • Offline registrations which consumes precious time and involves several stressful processes;

    (Quick online registration)

Other features include

  • School Timetable / Calender
  • Parent & Teachers Forum
  • School blog
